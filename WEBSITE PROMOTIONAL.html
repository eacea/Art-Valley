ABOUT OUR WORKSHOP

Key features of our workshop

About

This workshop is designed for people who want to engage their abilities in terms of digital arts and also for people who want to foster imagination and creativity.

Theme

The theme of our workshop is yet to be revealed as it is up to our attendees which helps them to be more interested and comfortable.

Goals

Our expectation is that as students leave, they will be enthusiastic, positive in their skills, and eager to learn more.

Participants

We welcome people from 12 to 15 because it would be easier for us to educate them and understand them better. This also creates an environment where they can be themselves without any problems and just overall fun without taking out learning.

ART VALLEY

We believe that art history is an essential element of understanding art as well as the perception of art. Through encouragement and support, we develop confidence in our participants.
